Frequently asked

Do you take commission on my orders?

No. Your ordering site and QR menu are commission‑free — you keep 100% of every order and own the customer relationship. You only pay your monthly plan.

Can I use my own brand and domain?

Yes. Map your custom domain, set your colors and logo, and both the guest ordering site and the staff console run fully white‑labeled as your restaurant.

Do you set up my menu for me?

We do. Send your existing menu or a PDF and we’ll build categories, items, modifiers and prices for you during onboarding — usually live within a few days.

Will it work for dine‑in, takeaway and delivery?

All three. One menu powers QR table ordering, counter takeaway and your own delivery — with a live kitchen display tying it together.

Can it run multiple outlets or cloud‑kitchen brands?

Yes. The Multi‑outlet plan manages many locations and virtual brands from one dashboard, with central menu, inventory and reporting.

Does it support GST billing and UPI/card payments?

Absolutely. Generate GST‑ready invoices, split bills, and accept cash, UPI and cards with reconciliation built in.
